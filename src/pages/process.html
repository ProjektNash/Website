How we work

Process Improvement

We don't just build things — we make sure what we build actually solves the right problem.

1

Discovery Call

A free 30-minute conversation to understand your business, your challenges, and what you're trying to achieve.

2

Process Mapping

We document your current workflows — spotting bottlenecks, manual tasks, and gaps where time or data is being lost.

3

Recommendations Report

A clear, jargon-free document outlining what we found, what we'd recommend, and the expected impact.

4

Design & Prototype

Before writing a line of code, we prototype the solution. You review and approve before we build.

5

Build & Iterate

We build in sprints with regular check-ins. You see progress throughout — no disappearing for months.

6

Handover & Support

Full documentation, training if needed, and ongoing support options so your team can run with it confidently.

💡

Why process first?

Building the wrong thing perfectly is still the wrong thing. Taking time upfront means the system we build actually fits — reducing rework and delivering real results.

⏱️

Typical timelines

Discovery & audit: 1–2 weeks. Small builds: 2–4 weeks. Larger systems: 6–12 weeks. We'll give you a clear timeline after the discovery call.

💬

Communication

Weekly updates as standard. Direct access to the person building your project — not an account manager.
